Home Fries

The recipe

This is a quick and easy potato recipe that I make everyday.

Ingridients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Ripe bananas
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Love

Steps

  1. Mix all ingridients in a bowl
  2. Butter bread pan
  3. Pour batter into pan
  4. Bake for 60 min for 350f
  5. Use toothpick to check cookness
  6. Eat up!
